Bee Hummingbird

Each night hummingbirds enter a hibernation-like state called torpor which helps them preserve energy.
  • Scientific Name: Mellisuga Helenae
  • Average Length: 5 to 6 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 3 to 5 years
  • Habitat: Cuban archipelago
The bee hummingbird, zunzuncito or Helena hummingbird. This species of Hummingbird is the world's smallest bird. More...
In flight it beats its wings as many as 80 times per second, which produces the 'humming' sound that earned the bird its name.
